JAKARTA INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION CENTER(JICC)

Jakarta International Convention Center (JICC), established in 1992, is a premier venue in central Jakarta, renowned for hosting prestigious international and local events. (former Jakarta Convention Center, JCC)

Located in the vibrant Senayan area, it offers versatile event spaces suitable for conferences, exhibitions, and cultural gatherings. With state-of-the-art facilities, excellent accessibility, and proximity to major business districts, hotels, and shopping centers.

JICC is a hub for seamless and successful events in the heart of Indonesia's capital. Its reputation and strategic location make it a top choice for global and regional events.

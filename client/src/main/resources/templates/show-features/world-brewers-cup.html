Competitors from around the globe will gather for the second World Coffee Championship of 2025, happening May 15-17 at the Jakarta World Coffee Championships (WCC), staged at World of Coffee Jakarta. The event includes the 2025 World Brewers Cup.

The World Brewers Cup is an international coffee brewing competition designed to showcase the craft and skill of manual filter coffee brewing.

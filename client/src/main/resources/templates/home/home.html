WORLD OF COFFEE ASIA MAY 7-9 2026 BANGKOK, THAILAND

WHY PARTICIPATE?

World of Coffee Asia is your gateway to Asia's vibrant specialty coffee culture and a golden opportunity to connect with the region's dynamic coffee industry. Whether you’re a roaster, barista, producer, or entrepreneur, this is your chance to engage with coffee enthusiasts and professionals at the heart of a coffee-producing country

Explore a bustling trade show floor, experience the rich traditions of Bangkok's coffee culture, and discover the latest innovations that are shaping the global coffee industry

Don’t miss your opportunity to expand your network, showcase your brand, and make your mark in the rapidly growing Asian coffee market!
